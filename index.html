What is Green Button?

Green Button is a secure way to get your energy usage information electronically.

Who's offering Green Button?

Today, more than 60 million households and businesses can use Green Button to access their own energy usage data from their electric utility, and a growing set of companies are offering products, services, and apps that use Green Button data.
